About Street Support

Street Support Network connects people and organisations locally to End Homelessness together.

Our long term vision is a citizen-led network, where people with personal insight into homelessness, decision makers and people with resources co-create solutions together.

We have a core team of 5 people based in Manchester, 2 of us have lived experience of homelessness. Each location we are active in has a local co-ordinator, supported by organisations and volunteers.

You can find out more in the News section of our website, or on Facebook, or Twitter.
